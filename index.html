Research Focus

I am a mathematician working independently on problems in number theory, with a specialization in Diophantine equations. My work focuses on explicit parametric constructions, particularly for the sums of three cubes problem and generalizations of taxicab numbers.

Current Work

My research involves developing closed-form symbolic generators for Diophantine equations, creating parametric frameworks for solving stubborn cases, and exploring connections between taxicab numbers and Pell-type equations.
